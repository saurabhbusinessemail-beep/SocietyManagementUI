Tenant Occupancy Setting

Configure how tenants are allowed to be added to this flat.

One tenant at a time

Adding a new tenant will automatically move out the existing tenant or owner. This is the standard behavior.

Default
Active

Multi tenant at a time

Allows multiple tenants to reside simultaneously. Adding a new tenant will not move out other tenants. Manual move-out is required.

Active

Note: Regardless of this setting, moving in as "Self" or setting the flat as "Vacant" will always move out all active tenants.
